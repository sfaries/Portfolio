Description

This project required me to build a type of guessing game. The computer picks a number between 1 and 100 at random and stores that number. The player then has to guess that number. After each guess, they are given feedback as to whether they are getting hotter or colder in terms of guessing the random number. The player also has the option to start a new game, which resets everything.
